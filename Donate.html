Thank you for considering a donation to Running Chicken – our work is only possible with your donations. All donations are 100% tax deductible. 

    At GlobalEyes, we believe that philanthropy is transitioning in time with our rapidly globalizing world, and our fundraising efforts match that belief. The sort of fundraising we do deviates from traditional conceptions about charities  you will not be invited to a gala, receive a paper-based mailer in your mailbox or answer a telephone solicitation call from us. Instead, we use an economically and technologically efficient, online, dynamic fundraising strategy that is comprised of a series of live goals – large and small, short and long term, ongoing and one-time – so that donors can pick and choose what to contribute to and how much to give.

Thank You!

Micro-goals are small fundraising goals that focus on one specific item, and will be introduced to our online donor community through e-newsletters. When the funds are raised, we'll set the next goal and send out another e-newsletter. As each goal is fulfilled, we'll follow-up with everyone who contributed to it with photos or video of what their donation helped to do.
